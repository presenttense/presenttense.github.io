Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I buy these mouthguards?

Our mouthguards are still in development. After they've been developed, they will undergo a period of strict testing. Subscribe to our mailing list to remain updated on our progress!

Why isn't there a picture of the mouthguards on the website?

See 'Why can't I buy these mouthguards?'

What are the natural mouthguards made of?

Our natural mouthguards are made from 100% pure natural rubber from the Hevea brasiliensis tree.

What's with the color of the mouthgurds?

Our mouthguards are made from pure natural rubber and contain no artificial colors. The amber color that you see is the natural color of the rubber.

Are your natural mouthguards eco-friendly?

Yes, natural rubber is considered a very sustainable raw material, as the rubber tree produces its latex sap for around 25 years. After that time, the wood from the trees gets harvested, and new trees are planted. Our mouthguards are even biodegradable, so they will not contribute to landfills!

Where are your mouthguards made?

In keeping with the theme of sustainability, our mouthguards are made very close to source of the natural rubber - in one of the world's leading rubber producing countries, Malaysia. The proximity of the rubber farms to the manufacturing plant cuts down on transportation emissions.

Should I wear your mouthguard if a have a latex allergy?

While you probably could (since we remove the latex allergy-causing protein during production), we recommend that you do not wear the mouthguards.

How often should I replace the mouthguard?

We recommend replacing the mouthguard every 4-6 weeks. There is no harm in continuing to wear it past that time, but at around 4-6 weeks, you may notice stickiness and discoloration in the mouthguard.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you are not satisfied with the mouthguard, send it back to us and we will refund your money.
